About Us

Have you ever wondered what it takes to go beyond the ordinary? All it takes is an idea! An idea that does not let you sit still but pushes you to go out there and break boundaries. Citrus Designs is based on one such "Archimedes-ish idea" that made the three of us collectively jump out of our bathtub(s) and run across the street screaming EUREKAAA!!!.

Okay..... we exaggerate. Sometimes. The idea is to design websites that are not just cool to look at, but are fun and awesome to use as well. Founded in November 2012, we are committed to being super-awesome to trigger an evolution, if not a revolution, in website design to help your company achieve that enviable cut above the rest.
